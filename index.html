What is Sell WBTC?

WBTC is Wrapped Bitcoin, an ERC-20 token representation of BTC on Ethereum; the official WBTC site is the primary project destination. This page opens Uniswap with WBTC preselected for a token swap and does not custody funds.

How Sell WBTC works

  1. Open the appUse the CTA to load app.uniswap.org with WBTC as the input asset and USDC as the output asset.
  2. Connect on EthereumConnect a wallet you control and make sure the selected network is Ethereum before approving anything.
  3. Review the quoteCheck output amount, route, price impact, slippage, and estimated gas; change the output token if needed.
  4. Confirm the swapApprove WBTC only if the app asks, then confirm the swap in your wallet after reviewing the final transaction.

Sell WBTC FAQ

How do I sell WBTC from a self-custody wallet?

Open the app link, connect on Ethereum, select an amount, review the quote, slippage, gas, and route, then confirm in your wallet. The swap only executes after you sign.

Is sell WBTC the same as redeeming WBTC for BTC?

No. A DEX swap converts WBTC into another Ethereum asset. Redemption to BTC is a separate merchant or custodian process, and requirements can vary.

What contract should I check before I sell WBTC?

Check the Ethereum WBTC contract at 0x2260FAC5E5542a773Aa44fBCfeDf7C193bc2C599 before approving a wallet transaction.

Can I sell WBTC for USDC instead of ETH?

Yes. The app link is prefilled for WBTC to USDC on Ethereum, and you can change the output token inside Uniswap before signing.

Are fees fixed when I sell WBTC?

No. Network gas, route, liquidity, price impact, and any app-displayed fees can change, so review the final quote before confirming.
